Features

Everything in one day

Not just another to-do app. Plani brings tasks, habits and your calendar together into a single daily view.

✔️

Tasks & lists

Create tasks with deadlines, priorities and checklists. Organize by life area. See what matters today — and what can wait.

🔁

Habits

Build daily routines that stick. Set schedules, track completions, use timers. Morning, afternoon or evening — your rhythm, your rules.

📅

Calendar

See your calendar events alongside tasks and habits. Find free time in your day and plan around what's already scheduled.

🏷️

Life areas

Organize everything by areas — work, health, personal, self care. Custom colors and icons. See where your time goes.

Balance rings

Each life area has a ring. Complete tasks and habits to fill them. When all rings move forward — your day is balanced. Not just productive, but whole.
